About Us

Cilantro Travel has passed a long way since its early beginnings in 1988.

The company was founded by two ordinary guys passionate about tourism. At the moment, the company serves more than 15,000 travellers across Europe each year.

Our professional team has been lucky to travel to a number of amazing countries across all continents and can proudly boast many years
of practical expertise in the travel industry.

They are committed to excellence in providing clients with high-quality travel and the best possible traveling experience at reasonable prices.

Due to the time spent abroad, they clearly understand all the demands of a modern traveler.

Our specialists are always friendly and pay
individual attention to each client. Expert guides will happily share with you all of their favourite spots.
